About Our Company

Delivering excellence in logistics since 2015

Our History

Founded in 2015 in Guntur, Andhra Pradesh, SRESHTA International Couriers began as a small local delivery service. Through dedication and customer-focused service, we've grown into a trusted logistics partner serving clients across India and internationally.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, expanding our service offerings and coverage areas to meet the evolving needs of our clients.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred logistics partner for businesses and individuals, recognized for our reliability, speed, and exceptional customer service.

We aim to continuously expand our network and leverage technology to provide seamless end-to-end logistics solutions.

Our Mission

To deliver packages with the highest level of care, efficiency, and reliability while maintaining competitive pricing and personalized service.

We commit to investing in our people, technology, and infrastructure to ensure we meet the growing demands of our customers.

Aerosols, lighters, etc.

Without proper authorization

Battery Products

  • Corpses, cremated or disinterred remains
  • Dry Ice (including that used for packaging)
  • Gambling Devices
  • Live animals and plants
  • Foodstuffs/Perishable items

Flammable, non-flammable, toxic

Dangerous Goods are materials or items with hazardous properties which, if not properly controlled, present a potential hazard to human health and safety, infrastructure, and/or their means of transport.
Toxic & Infectious

Substances liable either to cause death or serious injury or to harm human health if swallowed, inhaled, or by skin contact.

Radioactive

Any material containing radionuclides where both the activity concentration and the total activity exceed certain values.

Corrosives

Substances which, by chemical action, will cause severe damage when in contact with living tissue or other materials.

Miscellaneous

Other hazardous materials including environmentally hazardous substances, lithium batteries, etc.

Delivery timelines vary based on the service selected and destination. Domestic deliveries typically take 1-5 business days, while international shipments may take 3-10 business days depending on the destination country and customs clearance.
