Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my shipment?

You can track your shipment by entering your tracking number (starting with SIC) on our website's tracking page. We also provide tracking updates via SMS and email if you provided your contact details when shipping.

What are your business hours?

Our business hours are Monday to Friday from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM, and Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays.

How do I schedule a pickup?

You can schedule a pickup by calling our customer service at +91 94939 24742 or by filling out the pickup request form on our website. We require at least 2 hours notice for pickup scheduling.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, UPI payments (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), and bank transfers. For corporate accounts, we also offer net payment terms.

What items are prohibited for shipping?

We cannot ship hazardous materials, illegal items, perishable goods, live animals, currency, or other prohibited items as per government regulations. Please see our Prohibited Items page for complete details.
